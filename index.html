What is 99 Nights in the Forest?

99 Nights in the Forest is a multiplayer Roblox survival horror game that challenges players to survive 99 consecutive nights in a dark, haunted forest filled with danger.

By day, you gather resources and prepare for the coming darkness. By night, you defend your campfire from terrifying threats‚Äîespecially the mysterious and deadly Deer Monster. Along the way, you'll explore the forest, uncover secrets, and rescue missing children to unlock the game's true ending.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, 99 Nights in the Forest is completely free to play on Roblox. Optional in-game purchases like Medic or Ranger classes provide minor starting advantages but are not required to enjoy or complete the game.

The primary objective is to find and rescue all four missing children scattered across the map within 99 in-game days. Managing your time and resources effectively to complete this mission triggers the game's ending.

Bandages are rare, non-craftable items crucial for reviving teammates. The Medic class starts with two bandages. Otherwise, they can be found as rare loot in buildings, sheds, and crates throughout the forest.

Focus on building beds and rescuing missing children, as they grant time acceleration bonuses. This allows you to skip multiple nights at once, significantly shortening the total gameplay time.
